Top 5 Photography Tips for Beginners

1. Understand Lighting

Good lighting can make or break a photo. Learn to shoot during golden hour and avoid harsh midday sun.

2. Keep Your Camera Steady

Use a tripod or a stable surface to avoid blurry shots caused by camera shake.

3. Use the Rule of Thirds

This simple composition trick helps create visually appealing photos.

4. Practice Focus and Depth

Learn to focus on your subject and use aperture settings to control depth of field.

5. Edit, But Don’t Overdo It

Post-processing is key, but keep it natural. Learn the basics of color correction and cropping.
